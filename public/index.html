About Us

Welcome to our chat app! We are dedicated to providing a platform for individuals to learn programming languages and development skills in a fun and interactive way. Our app features a sign up and login page with authentication for secure access. Once logged in, users can join various rooms dedicated to different programming languages such as C, C++, Python, Java, Full stack Development and Data Structure. In addition to chat functionality, we also offer a feature to share location with other users. Our goal is to create a community of learners and developers who can exchange knowledge and support each other in their learning journey.You'll be provided a learning path too...

Thank you for choosing our app and we hope you enjoy using it!
